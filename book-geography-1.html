Preliminary Interpretation

This page is designed to demonstrate the analytical workflow rather than final results. The frequency table will show which spatial concepts appear most often, while the KWIC table will show how those words function in context.

The larger interpretive goal is to ask how geography education helped students imagine China as a legible national space: one structured by territory, administration, resources, borders, and transportation networks.
