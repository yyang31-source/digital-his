Geography Textbooks

This section presents geography textbooks as sources for studying how early twentieth-century students learned to imagine China as a territorial, administrative, and resource-based space.

The prototype begins with one scanned geography textbook and uses the remaining cards to show how the interface could expand into a larger textbook collection.
